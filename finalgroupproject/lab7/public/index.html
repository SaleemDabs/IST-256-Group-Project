Your Music List

Welcome to Your Music List. We are a web application that allows you to log your music journey. You can make a profile and add your favorite genres, artists, albums, songs, and concerts you have been to like an online diary.

Getting Started

  1. Create Account: Use the "Create Account" option in the dropdown menu to set up your profile.
  2. Profile: Access your profile via the "Profile" option to view and manage your saved items.
  3. Music Collection: Browse through the preset list of artists and albums. Choose your favorites and save them directly to your profile.
  4. About Us: Learn more about the team behind "Your Music List" via the "About Us" option.
